Our Services

GIS development

QGIS development - whether you need a simple Python plugin to speed-up your daily job or you want to develop a new C++ core geoprocessing pipeline we have the skills and experience to meet your requirements.

Training

Our international team of experienced trainers can deliver the best training on QGIS in Europe and in the US, ranging from introductory courses to advanced QGIS development both for the desktop and the server.

Ready for the web?

If you want to publish your maps and analysis we can provide you the most advanced Web GIS interactive applications and tailor them to suit your most demanding requirements.

Spatial analysis

Crunching spatial data to extract all kind of information from all sort of spatially enabled backends is one of our goals. We can help you to build your efficient geoprocessing pipelines for vector data and imagery.

Quality assurance

Our team has been providing QA services for QGIS for years, we can run manual and automated test on different platforms and fix bugs upstream on QGIS and on the most common open source GIS libraries.

Qualified support

Need help to find the best way to solve GIS processing problem? Is there any annoying bug slowing you down? We can help you with customized support plans for your company.
